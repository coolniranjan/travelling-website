Everest Camp Track

There are two base camps on Mount Everest, on opposite sides of the mountains: South Base Camp is in Nepal at an altitude of 5,364 metres, while North Base Camp is in Tibet, China at 5,150 metres.A busy hike, but an epic end result. If your in Nepal and have 2 weeks to spare then you need to go Everest Base Camp. You will feel a great sense of acheivement and you will experience the best views in the world. KedarKanth Camp Track

With a stunning height of 12,500ft, located within Govind Wildlife Sanctuary in Uttarkashi district of Uttarakhand, the Kedarkantha trek provides awe-inspiring views of the mountains and nature. The trek's uniqueness lies in the fact that it offers a peak summit and a lake campsite.Both side trekking distance of Kedarkantha trek is 20 km but steep is not easy to climb. If you are a good trekker then you can complete in 2 days too.
